COMPUTER ORGANIZATION:

The Computer Organization is concerned with the structure and behaviour of digital computers.The name objective of this subject is to understand the overall basic computer hardware structure,including the peripheral devices. Computer Organization includes the high level aspects of a design,memory system,the bus structure and the design of the internal CPU.
